About Professional Consulting Services, Inc

Professional Consulting Services (PCS) is a medical billing and management company based in Arkansas with offices in North Little Rock, Conway, Morrilton & Fort Smith. Having been in business since 1992, PCS has weathered massive changes in the healthcare landscape while remaining competitive and maintaining top of industry knowledge about the medical insurance marketplace. You can read more on their about page.

About The Project

Mission: This company had never had a website before and needed help with design and branding. We created mockups, did revisions, and got final approval from the client once they were happy with the design. I implemented the backend, and implemented the frontend pages using Tailwind CSS from the mockups.

Technologies Used: Laravel, Tailwind CSS

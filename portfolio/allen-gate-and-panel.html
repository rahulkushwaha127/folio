Allen Gate & Panel

About Allen Gate & Panel

Allen Gate & Panel, Inc. is a family-owned and operated business whose doors have been open for over 25 years. Starting as a personal project by Bill and Dick Allen in 1980, we have now grown to employ over 40 people in the small town of Prattsville, Arkansas where our products are manufactured. We strive to handle our customers with dignity and respect. No question or job is too small. Our business was built on taking care of individuals' needs and that is what we continue to cater to today. We handle a wide variety of cattle working equipment, from panels and gates to feeders and sweep systems. You can read more on their about page.

About The Project

Mission: Their previous website was built a long time ago. They needed to be on a more modern platform, with a fresh mobile responsive design. We also wanted to give them the ability to show product categories and individual products on their website. Phase 1 was built in a way where a more comprehensive quote system or ecommerence and checkout could be added in the future.

Technologies Used: Laravel, Tailwind CSS
